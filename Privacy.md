# Privacy Policy – MIFARE Chameleon Tools

**Last updated:** March 18, 2025

## Introduction

MIFARE Chameleon Tools is an iOS app that lets you read, edit, and write MIFARE Classic tags via a Chameleon Ultra device connected over Bluetooth. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

**MIFARE Chameleon Tools does not collect or send any personal data to external servers.**

All data you use in the app (tag dumps, `.keys` key files, block edits) stays **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us or to third parties.

## Data Used by the App

The app works exclusively with local data:

- **Tag dumps** – data read from MIFARE tags, either temporary or saved by you
- **Key files** – `.keys` files you choose to add or import into the app
- **Settings** – theme, language, and other preferences stored on your device

This data never leaves your iPhone or iPad unless you explicitly choose to save, copy, or share it.

## Bluetooth Connection

The app communicates with the Chameleon Ultra over Bluetooth. The connection is only between your device and your own Chameleon Ultra. No data from this communication is sent over the internet.

## Analytics and Tracking

The app **does not use** analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. No usage data, cookies, or advertising identifiers are collected.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this policy will be published on this page. The "Last updated" date indicates the latest version.
